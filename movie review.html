Review: ANIMAL

Synopsis

Animal revolves around the strained relationship between a powerful father and his emotionally starved son. The son grows up craving love and approval, but his father remains distant and strict. As he becomes an adult, the son transforms into a fierce and ruthless man. A sudden attack on his family forces him to return home and face enemies. In this journey, his bond with his father is tested through violence and emotions. The film explores love, anger, loyalty, and the price of being an "animal" in relationships
